Navi no Yado Hamabe（那維諾亞多漢瑪貝公寓）

從住宿步行 2 分鐘即可抵達海灘。 Navi no Yado Hamabe is set in Onna, 600 metres from Manza Beach, 8 km from Moon Beach, as well as 11 km from Maeda Cape. This apartment is a 12-minute walk from Cape Manza.

The apartment is composed of 1 bedroom, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and 1 bathroom. A flat-screen TV is available.

Mission Beach is 5 km from the apartment. Naha Airport is 39 km from the property.
