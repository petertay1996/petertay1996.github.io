Restay SaKuRa (Adult Only)（櫻花再住酒店（僅限成人））

Restay SaKuRa (Adult Only) provides accommodation in Hakodate. 5 km from Hakodate Goryokaku, the property is also 5 km away from Goryokaku Park. Hakodate City Office is 9 km away and Hakodate Park is 10 km from the love hotel.

All units in the love hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV. All guest rooms will provide guests with a microwave. Staff are available to help at the 24-hour front desk.

Miharashi Park is 4 km from Restay SaKuRa (Adult Only), while Trappistine Monasteries is 5 km away. The nearest airport is Hakodate Airport, 7 km from the accommodation.
